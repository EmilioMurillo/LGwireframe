WHERE GRIT MEETS OPPORTUNITY

At level ground(LG), urban youth cultivate grit and resilience through Mixed Martial Arts. They learn to appply these values 'off the matt' in the pursuit of their ambitions through our Student Trainer Certification and College Access Programs.
